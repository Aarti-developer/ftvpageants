FTV Pageants

INTRODUCTION

The world of beauty and fashion has many different styles and perceptions with everyday being a chance to redefine ourselves and rediscover ourselves. FashionTV brings you the opportunity of a lifetime to be the face that runs the place. We have invented a unique live TV event with the most incredible women, the most chiseled men and the most fiery young adults, an event known as the F PAGEANT INDIA. This title will be awarded to 8 people over the time frame of 30 events in 30 primary cities of our beautiful country towering over 1 year with a final megaevent that will be the belle of the ball for the entertainment and fashion industry.

PHILOSOPHY

VISION

We hope that each of these young men and women that choose to be a part of the F Pageant come away with much more than just a title, we hope they achieve the fulfillment of knowing they are role models in the community.

MISSION

To teach and encourage healthy living, self confidence,creative accomplishment, community service all towards the pursuit to achieve scholastic development and satisfaction.

AIM

We aim to not only look for potential but cultivate it by never letting anyone down. Our goal is to inspire, motivate and lift young adults all around the world.
